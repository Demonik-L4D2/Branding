THE CHRONICLES OF THE WASTES

The History of the Demonik Community

To the survivors, the veterans, and the newcomers: many of you joined this caravan at different waypoints. Some have been here since the first fire was lit; others found us in the storm. For your loyalty and your company, we are incredibly grateful.


This is our history—the record of how we survived the fall of an empire to build a home of our own.

I

The Age of Genesis

(2020)

In the fading days of 2019, a spark flickered in the dark. Ascension was born—not in a grand hall, but hosted on the humble machinery of its founder, a rig that hummed with the strain of our ambition. We were a small band of survivors then, unified by a singular vision of "Hardcore Co-op." We didn't want safety; we wanted a world where the shadows grew taller and the monsters grew stronger for every soul that dared to join the fight. It was fast, it was brutal, and it was unapologetically ours.

But even then, the foundation was cracked. The Founder held the keys to the kingdom but lacked the heart for its day-to-day governance. To keep the peace, the "Admin Rights" were distributed like candy—a gift of power that, in hindsight, sowed the first noxious seeds of discord. Egos clashed in the vacuum of true leadership, and the first fractures began to show in our armor.

II

The First Schism

(2021–2022)

As the seasons turned, the first great rift appeared. Differences in philosophy became impassable canyons, and the original council splintered. Many fled to start their own tribes, leaving Ascension to question its own soul. We tried to pivot, softening our jagged edges to welcome more travelers, but the "Original Sin" remained: a world without a dedicated architect is a world that eventually falls to ruin. The server performance degraded, the trolls invaded our sanctuary, and the reliability of our home began to crumble under the weight of neglect.

III

The Great Silence & The Shift

(2022–2024)

In 2022, a shadow fell over the record. Donuts, a central figure of the council, vanished into the mists for six months. This "shot over the bow" was a stark wake-up call to the Founder—a realization that a community is not just comprised of code, plugins, and servers, but a living, breathing pulse of people that requires care.

When he returned, the world had changed. The server was moved to a new host, and technical spirits were summoned to stabilize the land. But beneath the surface, a civil war of the mind was brewing. One side screamed for the return of the ancient "Old Ways" of hardcore brutality; the other sought a casual haven of mods and maps. Because the voices of the people were ignored, the cracks only widened, threatening to swallow us whole.

IV

The Purge of the Old Guard

(2024)

By mid-2024, the golden age was but a fading memory. The halls of Ascension were quiet, its elders inactive or gone. In the vacuum of leadership, a new power rose beside the Founder, whispering poison. This led to the "Great Split"—a final, bitter debate over the community’s future that would seal our fate.

The choice was made: the Founder surrendered the keys to a single autocrat. Overnight, the "Purge" began. Lifelong allies were cast out without trial, and the custom maps and configurations we had called home were burned to the ground in a desperate attempt to force a return to the past. Seeing the writing on the wall, Donuts—the last of the original council—walked away from the ruins, vowing to build something better.

V

The Rise of Demonik & The Path Ahead

(2024–2025)

We did not leave to start a war; we left to build a sanctuary. Demonik was forged on a sacred vow: that no single crown should rule. We are a collective, built on the bedrock belief that a community’s voice is its greatest strength, not a nuisance to be silenced.

We became a lighthouse for the exiled, the banned, and the ignored. Through the "Mass Exodus," our numbers swelled as refugees flooded our gates. While the road has been rocky and the nights long, we have built something far stronger than a mere server. We have built a brotherhood.

VI

The Horizon

(2026 & Beyond)

As we look toward 2026, the fires of our new home burn brighter than ever. We have weathered the storms of the past and emerged tempered like steel. Our gates remain open, and we look forward to welcoming even more of you into our ranks. Whether you are a veteran of the 2020 fires or a traveler who joined us yesterday, your story is now part of ours.

The chronicle is still being written. Let’s make the next chapters legendary.
