Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The Demonik Discord Bot collects limited information to provide its services. This includes Discord User IDs, usernames, and game-related statistics (such as kills, headshots, and playtime) from our L4D2 server.

We use this information to track player progress, display leaderboards, and manage community interactions. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely in our private database and is only accessible by server administrators for maintenance and moderation purposes.

4. User Rights

You can request to view or delete your stored game statistics by contacting a server administrator on our Discord.

5. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use tracking cookies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
